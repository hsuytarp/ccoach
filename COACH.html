The C.O.A.C.H is an interactive clinical assessment tool that will assist individuals ages 13 and older, and parents and caregivers of children ages 1 to 12 on deciding when to seek testing or medical care if they suspect they or someone they know has contracted COVID-19 or has come into close contact with someone who has COVID-19.The online, mobile-friendly tool asks a series of questions, and based on the userâ€™s responses, provides recommended actions and resources.

As the second strain of Corona Virus is on its peak across the tha whole word so, we need to be more alerted and alarmed. C.O.A.C.H is designed in such a way that it keeps you updated with the latest news of Corona Virus. C.O.A.C.H. is not only focused on Corona Virus, it is learning day by day so that, in the upcoming days it can help you in keeping yourself safe from different Viral and Bacterial diseases. Stay updated with C.O.A.C.H about the information of COVID 19 and other important Vaccines.
